Getting Started

Counter-Strike 2 is a team-based tactical shooter. The objective varies depending on the game mode, such as planting/defusing bombs or rescuing hostages.

Basic Controls

  • WASD: Move forward, left, backward, right
  • Mouse: Aim and shoot
  • Space: Jump
  • Shift: Walk silently
  • R: Reload
  • Number Keys: Switch weapons

Gameplay Tips

  • Communicate with teammates
  • Learn map layouts
  • Manage your economy wisely
  • Use cover and angles to your advantage
  • Practice aiming and reaction times

Game Modes

  • Competitive
  • Casual
  • Deathmatch
  • Wingman
  • Observe and Spectate
